# PGS Causal Inference

Research analysis of the Rutgers **Post-Graduation Survey (PGS)** estimating the causal effect of completing an internship on post-graduation employment, plus supporting exploratory and correlation analysis on GPA, salary, and major. The headline method is **Inverse Probability Weighting (IPW)** to adjust for background differences (GPA, school, major) and make the internship and non-internship groups comparable, as in a randomized experiment.

## Pipeline

`CleanPGS_2025.xlsx` + `GPAs.xlsx` → **`PGS.py`** → `Final24PGS.xlsx` → analysis scripts.

## Scripts

- **`PGS.py`** — Entry point. Merges the survey export with undergraduate GPA records on RUID, applies reference column labels, and writes the cleaned `Final24PGS.xlsx`.
- **`EDA.py`** — Exploratory analysis: response counts, GPA summary and by-school breakdown, employment-type and industry distributions, and salary summary.
- **`correlation.py`** — Computes and plots the correlation between GPA and annual salary.
- **`Causal_inf_emp.py`** — Core causal analysis. Estimates propensity scores via logistic regression, computes IPW weights, estimates the Average Treatment Effect (ATE) of internship on employment, and produces a Love plot for covariate balance.
- **`CausalInf_GPA.py`** — Plots salary distribution by GPA category (high vs. low) across the top 10 majors.

## Key Findings

- Internship completion is associated with a higher IPW-adjusted employment rate (≈38.7% vs. ≈32.3%).
- The Love plot confirms covariate balance improves after weighting (most SMDs fall within ±0.1).
- GPA's relationship to salary varies by major — a stronger signal in some fields than others.

## Inputs

- **`CleanPGS_2025.xlsx`** — Raw survey responses (`Raw Data` sheet).
- **`May25 Undergrad GPAs.xlsx`** — Undergraduate cumulative GPAs keyed by Rutgers ID.
- **Reference file** — Provides human-readable column labels.
- **`Final24PGS.xlsx`** — Generated by `PGS.py`; consumed by all analysis scripts.

> Data files contain student records and are **not** included in this repository.

## Outputs

Cleaned merged dataset, Matplotlib/Seaborn charts (GPA distribution, Love plot, employment bar chart, salary-by-major boxplots), and console summaries (ATE, GPA/salary statistics).
